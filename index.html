My name is Damian - Embedded Systems & Control Engineering enthusiast. I am currently pursuing Master's degree in Automatic Control and Robotics at AGH University of Krakow, focused on embedded systems and control-oriented hardware-software design.

My work centers on low-level C development for STM32 microcontrollers, real-time systems, CAN-based architectures, and custom electronics. I regularly use MATLAB/Simulink for modeling, system identification, and controller design, often deploying generated code to real hardware.

This portfolio documents selected academic and personal projects, including robotics platforms, measurement systems, power electronics, and experimental control setups - with emphasis on real constraints, testing, and iteration.
